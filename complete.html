The Seven

You have walked from stone to stone. You have spoken their years aloud. For a moment, their lives returned to them.

These seven were not the only ones. The cemetery holds a whole community — farmers and ministers, fishermen and mothers, children who never grew older than the year carved beneath their names. Every stone here is a story somebody waited to hear.

Thank you for listening.
